Privacy Policy

We take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose information about you when you use our tool of inventory management for gifting companies.

We use the information we collect about you to provide, maintain, and improve our tool. We also use your information to communicate with you, such as responding to your inquiries and sending you updates about our tool.

We do not sell or share your information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

We may share your information with our service providers who help us operate our tool. These service providers are authorized to use your information only as necessary to provide services to us.

We may also disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights or the rights of others.

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Choices

Contact Us
